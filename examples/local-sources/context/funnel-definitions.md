# Funnel definitions (synthetic)

The columns in `registrations_weekly.csv`, top to bottom of the funnel:

| Column | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| `visitors` | Unique visitors to the marketing site that week. |
| `signups_started` | Visitors who opened the sign-up form. |
| `registrations` | Visitors who completed registration. |
| `activations` | Registered users who reached the activation milestone. |

Healthy baseline conversion rates (pre-2026-03-09):

- visitor → signup started: ~20%
- signup started → registration: ~65%
- registration → activation: ~60%
